Development
FAQS
01
How do you transfer the IP for the software that you develop?
All Intellectual Property is owned by the client, as will be specified in our contract.
02
What programming languages do you use, and do you work with APIs?
e believe in using the right tool for the job, and as such we will use the most appropriate technology for your project. Our developers read up on the latest developments in their specialised fields, and attend development conferences to stay up to date. We work with most APIs (assuming that the documentation is in English or can be easily translated).
03
Can we meet the developers to discuss changes we want to be made?
Our developers and designers are normally in the office, so you can come and meet them personally, but any changes that you want to be made can be passed through your personal product manager that will in turn assign the query to the right developer.
How we develop your product
1
Get assigned a team of developers, where each specialises in a particular field.
2
Your personal team will be quick to act on feedback and will work tirelessly in collaboration with the designer team to provide creative solutions for your product.
3
The fully developed programme will be submitted to the project manager, that will scrutinise the end product before submitting it to you.
