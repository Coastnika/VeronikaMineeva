FAQS
01
How do you improve SEO?
In order to come higher up in Google Search ratings, we use a variety of tools such as writing good copy and organising website outreach. Our SEO team works closely with the development and marketing teams to make sure that they can move you up to the top of all the search engines.
02
Will my product come first for all google searches?
Your product only needs to come top in the searches that are relevant to your product so that only interested customers get to see your product. We can discuss the search terms that you would like to rank top for in our workshop session.
How does it work?
1
We decide the key search terms that we would like to rank top 5 for, which would preferably be a list of 10 terms all related to your product and on a similar topic area.
2
We work with the marketing and developer team to rank you higher up on the main search engines.
3
The final product will be submitted and can be adjusted in order to suit your needs.
